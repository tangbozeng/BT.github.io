2. What is the underlying biology

With the advantages of Next Generation Sequencing, we are able to explore more about what is going on when plants fight with pathogens. RNA-seq technology is to determine gene expression in a high resolution of quantitative format, which enables us to define the entire landscape of gene expression in both sides. It not only provides sensitivity to track dynamics of specific genes, but also delivers a real-time view about how pathogens control evasive growth, and how plants response and defense.


3. Discovery of resistance genes in plants against pathogens

Plants do not move, but they have capacity to perceive pathogens via multiple layers of defences. We are currently leveraging advanced technologies and resources to define a group of plant genes, enable plants overcome pathogens in a sustainable way.
