1. Co-expression network in M.oryzae genes

Magnaporthe oryzae is a model fungus to study plant-microbe interaction. By leveraging internal and external transcriptomic datasets, I am creating a bulked matrix to generate a WGCNA co-expression network for this specie. This would guide us to elucidate how the fungus governs its growth, its specialized structure called "appressoria", and its invasive growth in plants.
